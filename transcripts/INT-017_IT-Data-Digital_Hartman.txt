Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Everything we touch in GxP space needs computer-system validation, which slows every change
  down.
- We run the enterprise systems: MES, LIMS, ERP, the orchestration platform, and the
  validated-state paperwork around all of it.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. My inbox is a part-time job, triaging and routing email and pulling out the few things
   that actually need me.

2. Computer-system validation documentation is enormous, every system change in GxP needs
   validation protocols and reports written and executed.

3. There's spreadsheet sprawl everywhere, shadow tools doing critical work with no controls,
   and we spend time hunting them down.

4. Master data is maintained by hand across systems and it drifts, so reports disagree
   depending on the source.

5. Pulling data across our siloed systems for any cross-functional report is painful, MES,
   LIMS, ERP, and the orchestration platform don't talk, so we extract and join by hand.

6. Integrating the legacy systems properly needs real capital and a platform program, not a
   quick automation.

7. Service-desk tickets pour in and a person triages and routes every one, and a big share
   are the same handful of password and access issues.

Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Asked what they'd hand off first: the repetitive writing, no question.
