Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- We support the field team selling Amtagvi into ATCs, plus market access and the brand work.
- Small specialized field force calling on a defined set of treatment centers, not a primary-
  care blitz.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Competitive intelligence is a person reading press releases and conference coverage and
   writing it up, it's never current.

2. Promotional material has to clear medical-legal-regulatory review and the cycle is slow,
   lots of back-and-forth and version chasing.

3. Market-access dossiers and payer value decks are assembled from scratch each time even
   though 70% of the content repeats.

4. If we want more ATC coverage we need more field people on the ground, that's a headcount
   and territory decision.

5. KOL relationships are built on trust over years of in-person time. That's human, you can't
   automate a relationship with a thought leader.

6. Reps need a current picture of each ATC account, and today an analyst compiles it by hand
   from the CRM, ordering data, and emails before every cycle.

Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Flagged that whatever we build has to play nice with the validated systems or it won't get
