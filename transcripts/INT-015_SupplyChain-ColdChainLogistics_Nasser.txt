Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Chain of identity and chain of custody is everything. A single mislabeled handoff is a
  patient-impacting event.
- We orchestrate the cold chain both directions: fresh tumor inbound, cryopreserved product
  outbound to the ATC.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Every week I stitch together a status report from three or four different sources by hand,
   it's the same format every time and it still takes hours.

2. Every temperature excursion triggers a documented investigation, and writing those up and
   chasing the data is a recurring drain.

3. We're capacity-limited on LN2 dry shippers and qualified packaging. That's procurement and
   hardware, you can't software your way to more shippers.

4. Yield still varies batch to batch because it's living cells from a sick patient. No model
   fixes the underlying biology, the starting material is what it is.

5. Courier and vendor performance reporting is manual every quarter, pulling shipment data
   and building the scorecards by hand.

Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Flagged that whatever we build has to play nice with the validated systems or it won't get
  approved.
