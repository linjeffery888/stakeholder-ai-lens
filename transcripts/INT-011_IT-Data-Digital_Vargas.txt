Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Everything we touch in GxP space needs computer-system validation, which slows every change
  down.
- We run the enterprise systems: MES, LIMS, ERP, the orchestration platform, and the
  validated-state paperwork around all of it.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. There's spreadsheet sprawl everywhere, shadow tools doing critical work with no controls,
   and we spend time hunting them down.

2. Access provisioning is partly approvals and partly judgment about least-privilege, some of
   it has to stay human for compliance.

3. Computer-system validation documentation is enormous, every system change in GxP needs
   validation protocols and reports written and executed.

4. Finding the right SOP or work instruction is a daily hunt, they're scattered across
   SharePoint and shared drives and the search never finds the current version.

5. Integrating the legacy systems properly needs real capital and a platform program, not a
   quick automation.

6. Pulling data across our siloed systems for any cross-functional report is painful, MES,
   LIMS, ERP, and the orchestration platform don't talk, so we extract and join by hand.

7. Service-desk tickets pour in and a person triages and routes every one, and a big share
   are the same handful of password and access issues.

Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Says the team would welcome the help but worries about anything touching release decisions.
