----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Reviewing monitoring visit reports from CRAs is a steady load, reading long reports and
   pulling out the issues that need action.

2. Onboarding and keeping training material current is a constant background task, the
   documents go stale and nobody owns updating them.

3. Protocol deviation tracking across sites is manual reconciliation, and rolling it up for
   the sponsor oversight meeting takes a CTA most of a week.

4. Site startup drags on IRB and contract timelines that are external, no internal tool
   speeds up another institution's committee.

5. MSLs build their own slide decks for every center visit, pulling from the master deck and
   tailoring it, hours each.
