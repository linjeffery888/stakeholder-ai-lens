Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- We run the enterprise systems: MES, LIMS, ERP, the orchestration platform, and the
  validated-state paperwork around all of it.
- Everything we touch in GxP space needs computer-system validation, which slows every change
  down.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Access provisioning is partly approvals and partly judgment about least-privilege, some of
   it has to stay human for compliance.

2. Master data is maintained by hand across systems and it drifts, so reports disagree
   depending on the source.

3. Service-desk tickets pour in and a person triages and routes every one, and a big share
   are the same handful of password and access issues.

4. Integrating the legacy systems properly needs real capital and a platform program, not a
   quick automation.

5. So much knowledge is locked in people's heads and old email threads, when someone is out
   or leaves we lose the answer.

6. There's spreadsheet sprawl everywhere, shadow tools doing critical work with no controls,
   and we spend time hunting them down.

7. Computer-system validation documentation is enormous, every system change in GxP needs
   validation protocols and reports written and executed.

Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Asked what they'd hand off first: the repetitive writing, no question.
