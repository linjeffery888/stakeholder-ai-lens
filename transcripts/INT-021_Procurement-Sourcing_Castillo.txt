----------------------------------------------------------------
Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- A lot of single-source critical materials, which makes supply risk our constant background
  worry.
- We source everything from GMP raw materials and media to consumables and lab services.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Spend analysis across categories is a quarterly manual build, and without it we can't see
   where to consolidate or negotiate.

2. Supplier risk monitoring is reactive, we find out a supplier had a problem when a shipment
   is already late.

3. A lot of our critical GMP materials are single-source, and that supply risk is a market
   and qualification reality no software removes.

4. With sole suppliers we have almost no negotiating leverage, that's a market-structure
   problem, not an analytics one.

5. Purchase-order processing and three-way matching is high volume and manual, exceptions
   kicked out for a human to chase down all day.

6. Supplier onboarding and qualification is a paperwork marathon, collecting documents,
   quality questionnaires, and risk assessments for every new vendor.

7. Contract renewals sneak up on us because tracking them is a spreadsheet, and auto-renewals
   slip through and cost us.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Noted the volume is only going up as manufacturing internalizes and more markets come
  online.
