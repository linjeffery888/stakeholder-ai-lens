----------------------------------------------------------------

  approved or pending.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Keeping up with new guidances and summarizing what they mean for us is a reading-and-
   writing job nobody has time for.

2. Tracking regulatory commitments across all our markets is a spreadsheet that's always
   slightly out of date, and missing one is a serious problem.

3. Assembling submissions is heavy lifting, compiling modules, cross-referencing, formatting
   to each authority's spec. Hours of assembly per submission.

4. Some ex-US submissions need certified translations and local agent work that's inherently
   external and manual.

5. Audit and inspection prep is a fire drill every time, we scramble to gather documents and
   build the narrative from scattered sources.

6. Health-authority queries come in with tight clocks and we draft responses pulling from
   prior submissions and source docs every time.

7. The actual timing of FDA and EMA decisions is completely outside our control, we can
   prepare but we can't move their calendar.

Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Wants any solution to keep a human in the loop for anything patient-impacting.
