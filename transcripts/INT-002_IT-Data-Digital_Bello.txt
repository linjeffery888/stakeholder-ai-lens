Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- We run the enterprise systems: MES, LIMS, ERP, the orchestration platform, and the
  validated-state paperwork around all of it.
- Everything we touch in GxP space needs computer-system validation, which slows every change
  down.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Master data is maintained by hand across systems and it drifts, so reports disagree
   depending on the source.

2. Integrating the legacy systems properly needs real capital and a platform program, not a
   quick automation.

3. Service-desk tickets pour in and a person triages and routes every one, and a big share
   are the same handful of password and access issues.

4. Computer-system validation documentation is enormous, every system change in GxP needs
   validation protocols and reports written and executed.

5. There's spreadsheet sprawl everywhere, shadow tools doing critical work with no controls,
   and we spend time hunting them down.

6. Access provisioning is partly approvals and partly judgment about least-privilege, some of
   it has to stay human for compliance.

7. Pulling data across our siloed systems for any cross-functional report is painful, MES,
   LIMS, ERP, and the orchestration platform don't talk, so we extract and join by hand.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Flagged that whatever we build has to play nice with the validated systems or it won't get
  approved.
