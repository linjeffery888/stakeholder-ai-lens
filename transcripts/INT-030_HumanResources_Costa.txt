Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Writing and updating job descriptions is slow and they drift out of date, every manager
   wants a custom one.

2. Recruiting volume is high and screening resumes for manufacturing and QC roles is a huge
   manual sift, hundreds of applicants per req.

3. Onboarding is a documentation checklist nightmare, every new hire in a GxP role has a
   stack of forms and trainings to track to completion.

4. Interview scheduling across panels is pure coordination overhead, back-and-forth on
   calendars all day.

5. Training compliance tracking is manual, chasing people to complete required trainings and
   reconciling the LMS records.

6. Comp benchmarking decisions are judgment calls with real equity implications, I won't
   outsource that to a tool.

7. Employees constantly ask the same policy questions, PTO, benefits, travel, and HR answers
   them one at a time from documents that exist but nobody reads.
