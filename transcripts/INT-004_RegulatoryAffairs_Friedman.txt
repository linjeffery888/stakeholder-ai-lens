----------------------------------------------------------------
- We own submissions and health-authority interactions across the markets where Amtagvi is
  approved or pending.
- Lots of parallel markets now: US approved, Australia conditional, EMA resubmission in
  progress, Switzerland pending.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Tracking regulatory commitments across all our markets is a spreadsheet that's always
   slightly out of date, and missing one is a serious problem.

2. The actual timing of FDA and EMA decisions is completely outside our control, we can
   prepare but we can't move their calendar.

3. Labeling changes have to be managed across regions and reconciled against local
   requirements, very manual cross-checking.

4. Some ex-US submissions need certified translations and local agent work that's inherently
   external and manual.

5. Keeping up with new guidances and summarizing what they mean for us is a reading-and-
   writing job nobody has time for.

Closing note
