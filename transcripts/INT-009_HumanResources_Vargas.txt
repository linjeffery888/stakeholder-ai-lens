Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- We staff a very tight cell-therapy talent market: manufacturing associates, QC analysts, all
  hard to find.
- Headcount is growing with the manufacturing ramp, so recruiting and onboarding volume is
  high.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Comp benchmarking decisions are judgment calls with real equity implications, I won't
   outsource that to a tool.

2. Retention in this talent market is the real problem, people get poached and that's about
   comp, culture, and career, not automation.

3. Recruiting volume is high and screening resumes for manufacturing and QC roles is a huge
   manual sift, hundreds of applicants per req.

4. Onboarding and keeping training material current is a constant background task, the
   documents go stale and nobody owns updating them.

5. Training compliance tracking is manual, chasing people to complete required trainings and
   reconciling the LMS records.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Noted the volume is only going up as manufacturing internalizes and more markets come
  online.
