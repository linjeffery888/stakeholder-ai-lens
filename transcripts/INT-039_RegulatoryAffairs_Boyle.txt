----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Assembling submissions is heavy lifting, compiling modules, cross-referencing, formatting
   to each authority's spec. Hours of assembly per submission.

2. Health-authority queries come in with tight clocks and we draft responses pulling from
   prior submissions and source docs every time.

3. Keeping up with new guidances and summarizing what they mean for us is a reading-and-
   writing job nobody has time for.

4. Every week I stitch together a status report from three or four different sources by hand,
   it's the same format every time and it still takes hours.

5. Change control documentation is heavy, every change needs an impact assessment written,
   routed, and tracked, and the writing is repetitive.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- If I could wave a wand and kill one thing, it's the manual report stitching.
