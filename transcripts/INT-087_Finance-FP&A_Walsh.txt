----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Month-end account reconciliation is where it hurts, an analyst ties ERP balances to
   subledgers and spreadsheets and chases every variance. A material account is 90 min and we
   do 500-600 a close.

2. COGS per batch is the number leadership cares about most and allocating cost to each
   patient batch is a painful manual exercise across systems.

3. Building the management reporting pack is its own slog, copying numbers out of five
   sources into a deck every month.

4. Every contract that comes up I write a summary memo, pulling key terms, renewal dates, and
   pricing out of a long PDF into a one-pager.

5. Vendor and SaaS spend review is quarterly and manual, I go invoice by invoice looking for
   duplicate tools and licenses we forgot to cancel, and I'm sure we miss savings.

6. Revenue recognition for a personalized therapy has genuine judgment calls that I'm not
   handing to a model.
