Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- We staff a very tight cell-therapy talent market: manufacturing associates, QC analysts, all
  hard to find.
- Headcount is growing with the manufacturing ramp, so recruiting and onboarding volume is
  high.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Training compliance tracking is manual, chasing people to complete required trainings and
   reconciling the LMS records.

2. Employees constantly ask the same policy questions, PTO, benefits, travel, and HR answers
   them one at a time from documents that exist but nobody reads.

3. Retention in this talent market is the real problem, people get poached and that's about
   comp, culture, and career, not automation.

4. Writing and updating job descriptions is slow and they drift out of date, every manager
   wants a custom one.

5. Onboarding is a documentation checklist nightmare, every new hire in a GxP role has a
   stack of forms and trainings to track to completion.

6. So much knowledge is locked in people's heads and old email threads, when someone is out
   or leaves we lose the answer.

Closing note

- Flagged that whatever we build has to play nice with the validated systems or it won't get
