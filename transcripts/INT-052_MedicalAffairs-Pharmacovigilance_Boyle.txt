Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------

   and it's a manual scramble.

   human has to make the medical call.

3. Literature surveillance for safety is a firehose, someone screens new publications weekly
   for anything relevant to our product.

4. Adverse-event intake and case triage is high volume, reading in cases, coding them, and
   prioritizing, and the clock is regulatory so we can't fall behind.

5. My inbox is a part-time job, triaging and routing email and pulling out the few things
   that actually need me.
