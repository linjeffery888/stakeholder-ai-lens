Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- We sit between the prescribing center, the payer, and manufacturing. Nothing moves until
  benefits are confirmed.
- Team owns the patient journey from referral through scheduled infusion at the Authorized
  Treatment Center (ATC).

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Honestly we just don't have enough coordinators for the case volume coming. More ATCs
   onboarding means more cases and we're already maxed. We need heads.

2. There's no single tracker of which payers cover Amtagvi and under what criteria. Policies
   change and we find out the hard way when something gets denied.

3. Onboarding and keeping training material current is a constant background task, the
   documents go stale and nobody owns updating them.

4. A lot of this job is emotional support for patients who are out of options. That's human
   work, you can't automate sitting with someone through a terminal diagnosis.

5. Biggest time sink is benefit verification. Before we can schedule anyone a coordinator
   pulls status out of the payer portal, our case system, and the copay tool and hand-
   assembles one picture of the case. Call it 30-40 min each and we're running thousands of
   cases a year, and it's error prone if one source is stale.

Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Flagged that whatever we build has to play nice with the validated systems or it won't get
