Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- We run the trials: the sarcoma registrational study, endometrial, and the next-gen gene-
  edited TIL programs.
- Oversight of CROs and sites, plus all the trial documentation that has to be inspection-
  ready.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Reconciling CRO-delivered data against our own trackers is endless, the numbers never
   quite match and we chase the differences.

2. Reviewing monitoring visit reports from CRAs is a steady load, reading long reports and
   pulling out the issues that need action.

3. Slow enrollment is fundamentally a biology and epidemiology problem, the patients are rare
   and you can't manufacture eligible patients.

4. Enrollment forecasting is guesswork in a spreadsheet, and when a model would help, the
   underlying problem is still that these are rare tumors.

5. Actual safety signal detection takes clinical judgment, you can surface candidates but a
   human has to make the medical call.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Wants any solution to keep a human in the loop for anything patient-impacting.
