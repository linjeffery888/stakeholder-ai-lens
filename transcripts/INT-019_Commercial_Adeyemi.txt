Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Small specialized field force calling on a defined set of treatment centers, not a primary-
  care blitz.
- We support the field team selling Amtagvi into ATCs, plus market access and the brand work.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Reps need a current picture of each ATC account, and today an analyst compiles it by hand
   from the CRM, ordering data, and emails before every cycle.

2. CRM data hygiene is a mess, duplicate accounts, stale contacts, and someone is always
   cleaning it manually.

3. Competitive intelligence is a person reading press releases and conference coverage and
   writing it up, it's never current.

4. Market-access dossiers and payer value decks are assembled from scratch each time even
   though 70% of the content repeats.

5. Promotional material has to clear medical-legal-regulatory review and the cycle is slow,
   lots of back-and-forth and version chasing.

6. Off-label questions from HCPs are a compliance minefield, how we respond is a judgment and
   legal line, not something to fully automate.

7. KOL relationships are built on trust over years of in-person time. That's human, you can't
   automate a relationship with a thought leader.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Open to piloting something if it's reliable and stays inside our validated systems.
