Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Chain of identity and chain of custody is everything. A single mislabeled handoff is a
  patient-impacting event.
- We orchestrate the cold chain both directions: fresh tumor inbound, cryopreserved product
  outbound to the ATC.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. We monitor cold-chain shipments across multiple couriers and there's no single dashboard,
   so coordinators watch portals and emails and phone for status all day, both directions.

2. Every temperature excursion triggers a documented investigation, and writing those up and
   chasing the data is a recurring drain.

3. Courier and vendor performance reporting is manual every quarter, pulling shipment data
   and building the scorecards by hand.

4. Demand forecasting for consumables is basically a person's gut plus a spreadsheet, and
   when we're wrong we either stock out or sit on expensive expiring material.

5. Materials and consumables inventory never reconciles cleanly between the warehouse system
   and the ERP, so someone goes line by line monthly.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Flagged that whatever we build has to play nice with the validated systems or it won't get
  approved.
