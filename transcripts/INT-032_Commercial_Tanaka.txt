Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. CRM data hygiene is a mess, duplicate accounts, stale contacts, and someone is always
   cleaning it manually.

2. So much knowledge is locked in people's heads and old email threads, when someone is out
   or leaves we lose the answer.

3. Competitive intelligence is a person reading press releases and conference coverage and
   writing it up, it's never current.

4. Market-access dossiers and payer value decks are assembled from scratch each time even
   though 70% of the content repeats.

5. Field territory reporting is a manual monthly build, exporting from the CRM and
   reformatting into the deck leadership wants.

6. Reps need a current picture of each ATC account, and today an analyst compiles it by hand
   from the CRM, ordering data, and emails before every cycle.

7. If we want more ATC coverage we need more field people on the ground, that's a headcount
   and territory decision.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Skeptical that a tool can handle the GxP documentation rigor, wants to see it before
  trusting it.
