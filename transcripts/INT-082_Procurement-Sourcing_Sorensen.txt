Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- A lot of single-source critical materials, which makes supply risk our constant background
  worry.
- We source everything from GMP raw materials and media to consumables and lab services.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. With sole suppliers we have almost no negotiating leverage, that's a market-structure
   problem, not an analytics one.

2. Spend analysis across categories is a quarterly manual build, and without it we can't see
   where to consolidate or negotiate.

3. Supplier risk monitoring is reactive, we find out a supplier had a problem when a shipment
   is already late.

4. A lot of our critical GMP materials are single-source, and that supply risk is a market
   and qualification reality no software removes.

5. Contract renewals sneak up on us because tracking them is a spreadsheet, and auto-renewals
   slip through and cost us.

6. Supplier onboarding and qualification is a paperwork marathon, collecting documents,
   quality questionnaires, and risk assessments for every new vendor.

7. Building RFPs and comparing bids is slow, assembling the requirements and then normalizing
   wildly different vendor responses by hand.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Flagged that whatever we build has to play nice with the validated systems or it won't get
  approved.
