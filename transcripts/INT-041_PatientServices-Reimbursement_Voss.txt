Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Volume is climbing fast as more ATCs come online for Amtagvi.
- Team owns the patient journey from referral through scheduled infusion at the Authorized
  Treatment Center (ATC).

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Every week I stitch together a status report from three or four different sources by hand,
   it's the same format every time and it still takes hours.

2. Denial appeals are brutal. When a payer denies we draft the appeal more or less from
   scratch every time, people keep a few old letters and rewrite them, 45 min easy, and we do
   hundreds a year.

3. A lot of this job is emotional support for patients who are out of options. That's human
   work, you can't automate sitting with someone through a terminal diagnosis.

4. We burn hours just chasing prior-auth status, calling payers and sitting on hold to find
   out if something got approved. Pure waiting.

5. There's no single tracker of which payers cover Amtagvi and under what criteria. Policies
   change and we find out the hard way when something gets denied.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Open to piloting something if it's reliable and stays inside our validated systems.
