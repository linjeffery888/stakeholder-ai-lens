----------------------------------------------------------------
Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- We staff a very tight cell-therapy talent market: manufacturing associates, QC analysts, all
  hard to find.
- Headcount is growing with the manufacturing ramp, so recruiting and onboarding volume is
  high.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Recruiting volume is high and screening resumes for manufacturing and QC roles is a huge
   manual sift, hundreds of applicants per req.

2. Retention in this talent market is the real problem, people get poached and that's about
   comp, culture, and career, not automation.

3. Onboarding and keeping training material current is a constant background task, the
   documents go stale and nobody owns updating them.

4. Service-desk tickets pour in and a person triages and routes every one, and a big share
   are the same handful of password and access issues.

5. Writing and updating job descriptions is slow and they drift out of date, every manager
   wants a custom one.

6. Employees constantly ask the same policy questions, PTO, benefits, travel, and HR answers
   them one at a time from documents that exist but nobody reads.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Asked what they'd hand off first: the repetitive writing, no question.
