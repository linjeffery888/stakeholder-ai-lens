Background / current role
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Oversight of CROs and sites, plus all the trial documentation that has to be inspection-
  ready.
- We run the trials: the sarcoma registrational study, endometrial, and the next-gen gene-
  edited TIL programs.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Pain points / friction raised (in their words)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. So much knowledge is locked in people's heads and old email threads, when someone is out
   or leaves we lose the answer.

2. Slow enrollment is fundamentally a biology and epidemiology problem, the patients are rare
   and you can't manufacture eligible patients.

3. Drafting adverse-event narratives for the safety database is repetitive structured
   writing, and we do a lot of them.

4. Reconciling CRO-delivered data against our own trackers is endless, the numbers never
   quite match and we chase the differences.

5. Trial master file completeness is a constant audit risk, someone is forever checking which
   documents are missing or expired across thousands of files.

----------------------------------------------------------------
Closing note
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Open to piloting something if it's reliable and stays inside our validated systems.
